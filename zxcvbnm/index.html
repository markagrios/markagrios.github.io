book review: Principles of Neural Information Theory - James Stone

James Stone writes an amazing book for anyone wanting to explore more information theory in the context of neuroscience. This book only assumes basic linear algebra and some general mathematical familiarity in manipulating equations. Slowly walking through the different ways information theory can be applied to biological systems, Stone clearly explains the motivation and details of how and why information theory can be so effective, both theoretically and experimentally. close

The Bola Sphere table lamp by Pablo Designs

The Bola Sphere table lamp is an impressive piece of design from the Venezuelan studio Pablo Designs. My home is fairly warm and soft with light wood and fabric textures being the main elements so I often avoid polished metal but Pablo has created a lamp that fits perfectly in my living room and I'm sure it could fit in yours too, and I don't know you.
