Hi! I'm Mark Agrios. I live in Chicago and am pursuing my Neuroscience PhD at Northwestern University. I'm interested in computational, mathematical, and theoretical neuroscience as well just math by itself. Currently, I am working in the Miri Lab where we use various recording techniques to investigate skilled movement generation in mice. Using neuropixels, we can record from hundreds of individual neurons in different brain regions and study the activity as the animals move in real time.
