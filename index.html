About me.

As student from Washington D.C. I've found numerous places to fuel my never ending curiosity from building robots to performing magic on the streets. I've been involved with my high school's robotics team being co-captain three years and president my senior year. We compete in Botball and FTC and have won awards and honors for my code using color camera interpolation and infrared orientation. I have also been a member of my school TSA (technology students association) chapter for four years and was vice-president my junior year. We won second place for the animatronics event for the state of Virginia my first two years. Sometime before high school I taught myself the basics of performing magic and since then I have branched out to creating and publishing my own illusions and even hypnotizing spectators on the streets of D.C.
