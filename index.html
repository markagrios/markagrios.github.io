About me.

As student from Washington D.C. I've found numerous places to fuel my never ending curiosity from building robots to performing magic on the streets. I've been involved with my high school's robotics team being co-captain three years and president my senior year. We compete in Botball and FTC and have won awards and honors for my code using color camera interpolation and infrared orientation. I have also been a member of my school TSA (technology students association) chapter for four years and was vice-president my junior year. We won second place for the animatronics event for the state of Virginia my first two years.

I am a performing magician. I taught myself the basics of some card tricks when I was in middle school and was immediately fascinated with the combination of dexterity and thought that goes into fooling people. Since then I have explored other fields of magic from large stage illusions to hypnotism.

I can ride a unicycle.
