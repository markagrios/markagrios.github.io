Sheaves

Functional connectivity in mouse motor cortex

Sheaves are an incredible interesting concept in mathematics and there is a lot of potential applications for them in science. Unfortunately, the mathematical details are extremely dense and it is hard to gain an intuition for what's actually going on. So I wrote a paper doing my best to explain them in a very elementary way with lots of concrete examples and anaologies instead of the one-sentence definitions only intelligible by people who already know what a sheaf is. close
